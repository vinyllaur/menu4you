Our Café

Our vintage-style café will transport you back in time and welcome you with an admiring embrace. The aroma of freshly brewed coffee combines with the delicious taste of pastries, drawing you in from the street. Our café is more than just a place to have a cup of coffee. It's a great spot for a conversation, bury yourself in a good book, or simply relax and take in the ambiance. Whether you're a vintage fanatic or simply enjoy a touch of classic style, our cafe provides a delightful retreat from the daily.

About Us

Hello, Cafe enthusiast! We are MENU4YOU. We started from scratch, from selling our products on the streets and trying to promote it by giving our fliers. We worked hard and now we have our own cafe. We saw how our customers smiled through their eyes when they had our coffee and pastries! We have been serving our customers since 2022 and as time passes by we are growing and improving the products we can offer. We believe that great coffee and pastry makes someone’s day complete. Our team from MENU4YOU is a group of dreamers and coffee lovers who has one goal together and that is to serve great products and show good service to our customers.
